Elon Musk: The visionary

One of the most influential people on the earth, Musk is taking humanity into the future as we think of it.

Early Life

Elon Reeve Musk was born in Pretotia, the capital of South Africa, on June 28, 1971. After spending his childhood with his brother Kimbal and sister Tosca, at the age of 10, he developed an interest in computers. He was so much into computers that after teaching himself to program, he sold his first software(a computer game) called 'Blaster'; he was 12 years old then.

Education:

Musk attended the English-speaking Waterkloof House Preparatory School, and later graduated from Pretoria Boys High School.
At 17, Musk moved to Canada to avoid serving in the South African military, whose main duty in the late 1980s was enforcing apartheid. SAfter emigrating to Canada, Musk enrolled in Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario. He studied physics there.
Also he graduated with bachelor's degree in economics from the Wharton school of business.
At 24, he went to pursue his PhD. in applied Physics at Stanford University.

The birth of an Entrepeneur

Just after two days being enrolled into Stanford University, he dropped out to start his own comapany Zip2 which was an online city guide. It also provided content for new websites of both The New York Times and the Chicago Tribune, and in 1999, a division of Compaq Computer Corporation bought Zip2 for $307 million in cash and $34 million in stock options.

Also, in 1999 Musk co-founded X.com, an online financial services/payments company. An X.com acquisition the following year led to the creation of PayPal as it is known today, and in October 2002, PayPal was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion in stock. Before the sale, Musk owned 11 percent of PayPal stock.

Changing Today for a better Tomorrow

Having a keen interest in science fiction and commercial space travel, he founded the company Space Exploration Technologies or SpaceX as we know it. It aims to make space travel cheap and so much, so that the common public can afford it. Nasa now has contracts with SpaceX for most of its missions because it's cheap.
Recently, SpaceX has landed one of its rockets, made from reusable parts back on earth.
To combat the problem of climate change and non-sustainable energy, Elon Musk founded Tesla Motors which builds electric cars. The cars also have an autopilot system.
Other initiatives include Solar City, OpenAI, Neuralink and The Boring Company. All the ideas behind the project are wonderful and helps us imagine the future we want.


He is truly a remarkable person who aims to take humanity to the next level and make us live in a fully automated environment.
